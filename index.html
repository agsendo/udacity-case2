Travel Destinations for 2023

    Thailand

    Thailand is a vibrant and diverse country with rich cultural heritage.
    Some of the top destinations include Bangkok - the capital city that offers a mix of traditional and modern experiences, Chiang Mai - charming city known for its beautiful temples, night markets, and laid-back atmosphere, Ayutthaya - a historic city located just few hours from Bangkok, now home to numerous ancient temples and ruins, and Pattaya - a lively beach city that offers a mix of relaxation and nightlife.
    Thailand is also great place for outdoor ativities. The islands of Phuket, Koh Samui, and Koh Phi Phi offer some of the best snorkeling and diving spots. The beaches in Krabi are also popular for water sports, while Khao Yai National Park is perfect for hiking.
    Overall, Thailand is perfect destination for travelers who are looking to experience rich cultural heritage, amazing architecture, delicious food, stunning beaches or enjoy water sports.

    Japan

    Japan is a unique country with beautiful landscapes, unique cuisine, and a place where rich culture and traditions mix with technology and innovations.
    To get to know different sides of Japan, it is worth visiting some of the big cities as well as more rural places. Popular destinations are modern Tokyo, traditional Kyoto, Nikko with temples and shrines and Nara - first permanent capital of Japan with largest wooden building in the world and 15m statue of Buddha.
    Other places to visit are islands Hokkaido and Okinawa, the city of Hiroshima and Mount Fuji.
    For sports enthusiasts, Japan offers skiing, snowboarding as well as great choice of martial arts and sumo.
    And of course, there is so much delicious food to try: sushi, ramen, tempura, okonomiyaki (savoury pancake), matcha to drink or tea at traditional teahouse.

    Morocco

    Morocco is a country of exotic beauty, where ancient history and culture come together with vibrant colours and flavours.
    To experience the amazing variety, visitors can spend time at the busy markets of Marrakech, explore the UNESCO medieval city of Fez, and wander through the “blue city” of Chefchaouen.
    Morocco also offers natural attractions - the most popular are the landscapes of Sahara Desert with camel rides, Atlas Mountains, or beaches of Essaouira.
    The country is also known for its delicious cuisine that has Berber, Arab and Mediterranean influences. Some of the dishes to try are tagine (stew of meat and vegetables), couscous, harira (soup with lentils, chickpeas and spices), pastilla (savoury pie) and traditional mint tea.
    With its warm hospitality and a unique blend of cultures, Morocco is a great destination to visit.

    Norway

    Norway is an amazing country with some of the most beautiful natural scenery in the world, but also rich in culture and traditions.
    Some of the best nature destinations include the fjords, such as the Geirangerfjord and the Sognefjord, that are also great for hiking and kayaking. There aro also many national parks, such as Jotunheimen and Rondane, with stunning landscapes and wildlife. For visitors coming between September and April, it is possible to experience the Northern Lights.
    For culture, the city of Oslo is the main destination in Norway, with its museums and galleries: Munch Museum, Opera House, Vigeland Park and the Royal Palace.
    When it comes to traditions, most popular include folk festivals, medieval reenactments and bonfires as well as the Viking heritage. The typical food consists mostly of fresh or dried fish, lamb stew and cloudberries.

    Peru

    Peru is home to some of the most spectacular archaeological sites in the world. It's also a great destination for foodies, with a diverse range of delicious dishes to try.
    Some must-see places to visit are the ancient ruins of Machu Picchu (one of the New Seven Wonders of the World !!), Cusco city (historic capital of the Inca Empire), Lake Titicaca, Colca Canyon, Nazca Lines (ancient geoglyps of animals, humanoids and geometric patterns), and Amazon Forest (with hikes, boat trips and wildlife spotting).
    Peru has also amazing selection of food such as ceviche (refreshing dish made with raw seafood), beef stir-fry, chicken marinated in spices, and for a drink coca tea or Pisco Sour (made wing grape brandy).
    Peru is an amazing destination to discover captivating ancient ruins, nature and culinary delights.
